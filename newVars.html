New Variables

Table 1: New Spotify data variables
variable class range description
classicalness double 0-1 an average of acousticness, instrumentalness, and 1-speechiness approximating the amount the song could be described as classical, higher scores imply more classical
title.words double 1-26 the number of words in the song title
norm.loudness double 0-1 a min-max normalized spotify_songs$loudness
norm.tempo double 0-1 a min-max normalized spotify_songs$tempo
intensity double 0-1 the average of energy, normalized tempo, and 1- normalized loudness, where higher scores imply higher intensity
livetrack double 0-1 Livetrack is a categorization of tracks as performed live in front of an audience (livetrack=1) or not performed live (livetrack=0). Livetrack is created from the liveness variable, in which songs with a value of liveness > 0.80 are likely to have been performed in front of an audience, whereas songs with a value of liveness < 0.80 are less likely to have been performed in front of an audience.
duration_mins double 0.07-8.6 minutes The duration of song in minutes, converted from milliseconds (duration_ms)
speechiness_cat double 0-2 Speechiness_cat is a categorization of tracks as non-speech-like (0), tracks with music and speech (1), and spoken word tracks (2). Speechiness_cat is created from speechiness, in which tracks with a value < 0.33 are likely non-speech tracks, tracks with a value >0.33 or <0.66 are likely music and speech, and >0.66 are likely spoken word tracks.

Table 1 describes 8 new variables, classicalness, title.words, norm.loudness, norm.tempo, itensity, livetrack, duration_mins, speechiness_cat, created from the Spotify dataset. The table provides the class, range, and description of each new variable.
